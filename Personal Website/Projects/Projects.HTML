Under His True Light Google Marketing Campaign - UT at Dallas Academic Project


This project was meant to better optimize her landing page, however we decided to take that to the next level and overhauling her current Google campaigns. After a close analysis and keyword research we added various negative exact/phrase and phrase match keywords to increase traffic, but reduce bounces at the same time. We also requested spam backlinks to be removed. Overall, her SEO score improved from a poor score of 21 to a moderate score of 53 and an exponential increase in clicks (43%) and impressions (57%) after implementing the new Google Ad campaign.
